# StitchLens Executive Summary

### Vision
Transform personal photos into beautiful, stitch-ready needlepoint designs — empowering hobbyists to create custom art with ease.

### Mission
Make custom needlepoint design as easy as uploading a photo — combining technology, creativity, and craftsmanship in a single web platform.

### Product Overview
- Web-based tool that converts images into printable needlepoint charts.  
- Outputs include color-reduced stitch grids, yarn legends, and PDF templates.  
- Phase 2: printed canvas + yarn kit fulfillment via partner dropship model.  
- Future: user accounts, community marketplace, and mobile app.

### Target Market
- Primary: North American needlepoint hobbyists (mostly female, 40–75).  
- Secondary: cross-stitch and embroidery enthusiasts, small design studios.  
- Market size: ~$500M+ annual needlepoint-related sales (US/Canada).

### Competitive Edge
- Modern, self-serve UI for instant pattern creation.  
- Accurate color mapping to major yarn brands (DMC, Appleton, etc.).  
- Scalable digital-first model with optional physical fulfillment.  

### Monetization
- **Freemium model:** free previews, paid PDF exports.  
- **Pro subscription:** unlimited exports + palette management.  
- **Kit sales:** printed canvas + yarn via dropship partners.  
- **Marketplace:** commission on creator pattern sales.

### Brand Identity
**Name:** StitchLens  
**Tone:** Friendly, creative, hobbyist-first, feminine but modern.  
**Tagline:** *From photo to pattern — stitch your story.*

### Growth Plan
| Phase | Key Focus | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| MVP | Upload → PDF Pattern Flow | 6 months |
| V2 | Accounts + Saved Projects | 12 months |
| V3 | Printed Canvas & Kits | 18–24 months |
| V4 | Marketplace & Community | 24–36 months |

### High-Level Financial Outlook
- Year 1: $30K revenue (early adopters)  
- Year 2: $280K revenue (subscriptions + kits)  
- Year 3: $1.5M revenue (global scale)  
- 60–70% gross margins; minimal overhead through automation.
