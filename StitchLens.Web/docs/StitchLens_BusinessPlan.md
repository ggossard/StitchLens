# StitchLens Business Plan (Startup Deck Style)

## 1. Executive Summary
- **Vision:** Empower hobbyists to transform photos into custom, stitch-ready needlepoint designs in minutes.  
- **Tagline:** *From photo to pattern — stitch your story.*  
- **Business Model:** Digital-first platform offering downloadable charts, yarn color mapping, and optional printed canvas & kit fulfillment (Phase 2).  
- **Launch Focus:** Web application targeting North American needlepoint hobbyists; scalable worldwide.  
- **Ownership:** Privately owned and operated by founder (contractor-supported for tech and fulfillment).  

---

## 2. Problem & Opportunity
**Problem:**  
- Converting images into usable needlepoint patterns is complex, technical, and expensive.  
- Current online tools lack realism, accurate yarn mapping, or kit integration.  
- Hobbyists often resort to manual or paid custom design services.

**Opportunity:**  
- Provide a fast, self-serve web app that bridges art and craft.  
- Create a one-stop shop: design → color match → print → stitch.  
- Capture recurring revenue through subscriptions, pattern sales, and fulfillment partnerships.

---

## 3. Product Overview
**MVP Features:**  
- Upload or drag-drop a photo  
- Crop and scale to desired dimensions  
- Choose mesh count, stitch type, and color limit  
- Generate color-reduced preview + yarn mapping (e.g., DMC, Appleton)  
- Export printable PDF chart and color legend  

**Future Phases:**  
- Printed canvas + yarn kits via fulfillment partner  
- Community pattern sharing & marketplace  
- User accounts with saved projects and palettes  
- Mobile-friendly version (tablet emphasis)  

---

## 4. Target Market & Audience
**Primary Audience:**  
- North American hobbyists (predominantly female, 40–75, discretionary income, passionate about needle arts).  

**Secondary Audience:**  
- Cross-stitch and embroidery enthusiasts seeking custom design tools.  
- Designers offering boutique canvas commissions.  

**Market Size (North America):**  
- ~2.4M active needlepoint participants.  
- ~7.1M total counted-thread enthusiasts (including cross-stitch).  
- Estimated $500M+ annual needlepoint supply market (canvases, yarns, accessories).  

**Positioning:**  
- Accessible, friendly, hobbyist-centric brand that blends creativity with convenience.  

---

## 5. Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Focus | Gaps |
|-------------|--------|------|
| **NeedlePaint.com** | Custom printed canvases | No lightweight DIY pattern tool |
| **Pic2Pat** | Free cross-stitch generator | Outdated interface, no yarn mapping |
| **Anne Fisher / Pixels on Cotton** | High-end printed canvases | Not self-service |
| **Hedgehog Needlepoint** | Boutique printed canvases | Manual order flow |

**Differentiator:**  
- Intuitive, modern UI + accurate yarn mapping + automated pattern generation.  
- Future: one-click fulfillment with partners.  

---

## 6. Monetization Model
| Channel | Description | Est. Unit Revenue |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Freemium Downloads** | Watermarked preview PDFs | $0 |
| **Paid Pattern Exports** | High-res, full-detail PDFs | $5–15 per design |
| **Subscription (Pro)** | Unlimited exports, saved projects, advanced palettes | $8.99/month |
| **Printed Canvas (Dropship)** | Partner-fulfilled, you retain markup | 20–30% gross margin |
| **Full Kit Upsell** | Canvas + yarn bundle | 25–35% gross margin |
| **Marketplace Commissions** | Designer-sold patterns, revenue share | 10–15% |

---

## 7. Go-to-Market Strategy
**Launch Plan:**  
- Phase 1: Beta web app with simple upload → PDF export flow.  
- Phase 2: Partnerships with printing & yarn fulfillment vendors.  
- Phase 3: Creator marketplace and affiliate programs.

**Marketing Channels:**  
- Facebook & Pinterest ads targeting “needlepoint” and “cross stitch” interests.  
- Collaborations with craft influencers and YouTube creators.  
- Blog + SEO: “turn your photo into needlepoint pattern.”  
- Email marketing for project inspiration & seasonal campaigns.  

---

## 8. Operations Model
**Fulfillment:**  
- Dropship/low-inventory model with vetted partners (NeedlePaint, Hedgehog, etc.).  
- Digital products fully automated (pattern generation, payments, delivery).  

**Customer Service:**  
- Email/chat support for order tracking and pattern questions.  
- Automated FAQs and community forum (Phase 2).  

**Team Model:**  
- Founder-operated with part-time contractors (software, marketing, customer service).  
- Scalable to add fulfillment coordinator as order volume grows.  

---

## 9. Technology & Scalability
- Cloud-based web architecture with modular back end (future mobile extension).  
- Scalable compute for image processing.  
- Multi-language ready (i18n from launch).  
- Vendor integration via APIs (Stripe, print partner, shipping).  
- PDF and image rendering pipeline designed for batch processing.
